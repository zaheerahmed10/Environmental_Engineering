Admissions

Bachelor of Science in Environmental Engineering (BSEE)

Eligibility Criteria:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Strong background in Mathematics and Sciences
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
  • SAT/ACT scores (optional for 2024)
  • Letter of recommendation
  • Personal statement

Application Requirements:

  • Completed online application form
  • Official transcripts
  • $50 application fee
  • English proficiency test scores (for international students)

Important Dates:

  • Fall Semester Deadline: June 15
  • Spring Semester Deadline: November 15
  • Summer Session Deadline: March 15

Master of Science in Environmental Engineering (MSEE)

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering or related field
  • Minimum GPA of 3.2 on a 4.0 scale
  • GRE scores
  • Research proposal
  • Two letters of recommendation
