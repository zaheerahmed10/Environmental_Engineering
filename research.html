Research Areas

💧 Water Quality & Treatment

Advanced water purification technologies, wastewater treatment, desalination, and water resource management.

Key Projects: Membrane filtration, UV disinfection, Nanotechnology in water treatment

🌫️ Air Pollution Control

Atmospheric monitoring, pollution mitigation strategies, air quality modeling, and emission control technologies.

Key Projects: Particulate matter reduction, VOC control, Urban air quality

🌡️ Climate Change & Adaptation

Climate modeling, impact assessment, mitigation strategies, and adaptation technologies.

Key Projects: Carbon capture, Climate resilience, Green infrastructure

♻️ Sustainable Waste Management

Circular economy, waste-to-energy, recycling technologies, and hazardous waste treatment.

Key Projects: Plastic recycling, E-waste management, Biogas production

☀️ Renewable Energy Systems

Solar, wind, hydro, and bioenergy systems, energy efficiency, and sustainable infrastructure.

Key Projects: Solar thermal systems, Wind farm optimization, Smart grids

🌱 Environmental Biotechnology

Bioremediation, bioenergy, genetically engineered organisms for environmental applications.

Key Projects: Microbial fuel cells, Phytoremediation, Enzyme technology
