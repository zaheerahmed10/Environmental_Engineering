About the Department

Our Vision

To be a global leader in environmental engineering education and research, creating innovative solutions for a sustainable world.

Our Mission

  • Provide exceptional education in environmental engineering
  • Conduct cutting-edge research addressing environmental challenges
  • Foster collaboration with industry and communities
  • Promote sustainable practices and environmental stewardship
  • Prepare students for leadership roles in environmental protection

Department History

Established in 1990, our department has been at the forefront of environmental education and research for over three decades. We have produced thousands of graduates who are making significant contributions to environmental protection worldwide.
